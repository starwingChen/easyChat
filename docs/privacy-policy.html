Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2026

At easyChat, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our easyChat application and related services (collectively, the "Services").

1. Core Privacy Principles

We operate under the principle of Data Minimization. Our architecture is designed to ensure that your private conversations remain under your control.

  • Local Storage: We do not store your chat history or conversation content on our servers. All chat logs are saved exclusively in your browser’s local storage.
  • No AI Training: We do not use your chat data, inputs, or personal information to train, improve, or fine-tune AI models.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a secure and reliable service, we may collect limited categories of information:

  • Technical Metadata: We may automatically collect certain technical information, including IP addresses, device types, operating system versions, and browser types. This data is used solely for security auditing, preventing abuse, and server diagnostics.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication, account management, and essential service notifications.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymized usage data (e.g., feature engagement metrics) to improve user experience. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. Third-Party Services and Data Transfers

To facilitate AI-powered interactions, your data may be processed as follows:

  • Model Providers: Your real-time inputs are transmitted via encrypted protocols (HTTPS) to third-party AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic). These providers receive the data only to generate a response. We ensure that our integration adheres to professional privacy standards that opt-out of data training.
  • No Third-Party Selling: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party marketers or data brokers.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All communication between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL (HTTPS).
  • User Responsibility: Since your chat history is stored locally in your browser, the security of that data also depends on the security of your device. We recommend clearing your browser data if using a shared or public computer.

5. Your Rights and Control

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Erasure: You can delete all chat history instantly by using the "Clear All Data" function within the app.
  • Data Portability: We provide tools to export your local chat history in JSON or Markdown formats.
  • Account Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account and any associated metadata at any time.

6. Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your information only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation or government request.
  • Protect and defend the rights or property of easyChat.
  • Protect the personal safety of users or the public.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by posting a notice on our website or by sending you an email. Your continued use of the Services after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
